OUR MISSION

We strive to ensure the best for our users. Inspired by the statistics demonstrating unhealthy eating habits in the US, we created "What DIET" which incorporates our core values of healthy eating and support for people who wish to start diets. "What DIET" provides a safe space where people can be part of a community that supports, guides, and helps them stay healthy while maintaining proper nutritional necessities.
