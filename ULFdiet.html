Ultra Low Fat Diet

   

The Ultra - Low - Fat - Diet is a diet that restricts consumption of high-fat foods from certain animal, plant, and manufactured sources. Some suggested foods to eat on this diet are: Fruits of almost every variety, Leafy green vegetables, Legumes, Ancient grains, Chicken breast, White fish, Low-fat dairy Egg whites.

Some restricted foods consist of: Dairy,Meat,Certain animal-derived and plant-derived cooking oils, Processed foods, and Anything deep-fried. It tends to be low in protein and very high in carbs. Studies show that an ultra-low-fat diet may have very impressive benefits against several serious conditions.

Suggested Foods

  • Leafy Greens
  • Fruits
  • Sweet potatoes
  • White fish
  • Chicken Breast
  • Low fat dairy
  • Vegetables
  • Egg Whites
  • Tofu

Restricted Foods

  • Red Meats
  • Butter and certain oils
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salad Dressings
  • Gravies and sauces
  • Limited Chocolate
