Paleo Diet

   

The paleo diet is a plant based diet and lean protein diet. It excludes processed foods such as sugar,dairy, and grains. Some choose this diet because they want to return back to the meal plans of our early ancestors. While some choose paleo because they want to eat fewer calories and lose weight.

Benefits: The lack of processed foods in this diet can you feel more energetic, less inflammation, improved heart health, and increased insulin sensitivity. This can lower cholesterol and reduce the risk of heart disease.

Risks: Some of the risk of this diet is deficiencies in calcium and vitamin D which could lead to issues in bone health. If saturated fat and protein is consumed above recommended levels then it leads to increased risk of kidney, heart disease, and certain cancers.

Suggested Foods

  • Meat
  • Fish and seafood
  • Eggs
  • vegetables
  • fruits
  • nuts
  • healthy fats
  • oils

Restricted Foods

  • Cereal Grains
  • Legumes (peanuts, beans, lentils, tofu)
  • Refined sugar
  • Processed foods
  • Soda and sweet beverages
  • Refined vegetable oils
  • Salt
  • Artificial sweetners
